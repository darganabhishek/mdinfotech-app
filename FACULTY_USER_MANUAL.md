# 🎓 Faculty User Manual
## M.D. INFOTECH - Institute Management System

Welcome, Faculty member. This manual will guide you through the features designed to help you manage your classes, track attendance, and provide resources to your students.

---

## 1. 🕐 Faculty Clock-In / Clock-Out
As part of our attendance and payroll policy, all faculty members must record their arrival and departure daily.

- **How to Use**:
  1. Navigate to **Secure Attendance > Faculty Clock**.
  2. **Face Capture**: Click "Open Camera" and take a clear photo of your face.
  3. **GPS Verification**: Ensure your device's location services (GPS) are enabled.
  4. **Clock In**: Click the "Clock In" button when you arrive.
  5. **Clock Out**: Click the "Clock Out" button when your shift ends.
- **Verification**: The system uses **Face ID** and **GPS Geofencing** to verify your presence at the institute.

---

## 2. 📡 Marking Student Attendance
You have two ways to mark student attendance:

### A. Start QR Session (Recommended)
This generates a live QR code that students scan via their own mobile portals.
1. Go to **Secure Attendance > Start QR Session**.
2. **Select Batch**: Choose the current batch from the dropdown (grouped by time slot).
3. **Start Session**: Click "Start Session & Generate QR".
4. **Student Scanning**: Show the QR code on your screen to the class. Students will scan it to mark themselves present.
5. **Real-time Tracking**: You can see a live count of students who have marked themselves present.
6. **End Session**: Remember to click "End Active Session" once everyone has scanned.

### B. Attendance Hub (Manual)
If students don't have devices or for older records, use the manual hub.
1. Go to **Management > Attendance Hub**.
2. Select the **Batch** and **Date**.
3. For each student, click **Present**, **Absent**, or **Late**.
4. (Optional) Add notes for specific students (e.g., "Medical leave").
5. Click **Save Attendance** at the top right.

---

## 3. 📅 Schedule & Batches
- **Dashboard**: Your dashboard shows your **Active Batches** and **Today's Schedule**.
- **Timetable**: View your assigned classes and rooms for the entire week.
- **Batches**: View the list of students enrolled in each of your batches to track their progress.

---

## 4. 📚 E-Learning Hub
Manage the resources and study materials for your courses.
1. Navigate to **Academics > E-learning Hub**.
2. **Upload Resources**: Add PDF notes, video links, or assignment descriptions for specific courses.
3. Your students will see these materials instantly in their respective portals.

---

## 🔒 Security Best Practices
- **Do not share QR codes**: The attendance QR codes refresh every 10-30 seconds and are location-locked. Encourage students to scan while in the classroom.
- **Location Permissions**: Always allow the browser to access your location for the Faculty Clock and QR sessions to work correctly.
- **Secure Logouts**: Always sign out of your account if you are using a shared institute computer.
